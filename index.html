Welcome to an Overview of the Mandolin

I'm no expert on the Mandolin, but for years, I've loved the look, sound, and history of this underappreciated instrument.
Therefore, as an act of devotion to my part-time hobby, I've put together this website to create a reservoir for Mandolin information.
The second purpose for this site is to work on web development skills.
